Our Story

Founded in 2008, HireVedic Advisory LLP began with a vision to transform how organizations approach talent management. Our founders recognized that traditional HR practices were failing to keep pace with rapidly evolving business landscapes.

Starting with a small team of experienced consultants, we've grown into a global advisory firm with offices in 12 countries, serving clients across industries from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Today, we combine our deep industry expertise with cutting-edge analytics and innovative methodologies to deliver transformative talent solutions that drive measurable business outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help with our talent needs?

We typically begin with a discovery phase that takes 1-2 weeks, followed by strategic planning. Implementation timelines vary based on complexity, but we pride ourselves on delivering rapid results while ensuring quality and sustainability.

Do you work with companies of all sizes?

Yes, we serve organizations ranging from fast-growing startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our solutions are tailored to your specific needs, challenges, and growth stage, ensuring relevance regardless of company size.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we work across industries, we have deep expertise in Financial Services, Healthcare, Technology, Manufacturing, Retail, and Professional Services. Our consultants bring industry-specific knowledge to every engagement.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear metrics at the beginning of each engagement, aligned with your business objectives. Common success measures include time-to-hire, employee engagement, retention rates, internal promotion rates, and ROI on talent initiatives.
